Instead of adding right to left (as taught in school), add from left to right mentally. Your brain holds bigger numbers more naturally this way.

Sutra: Nikhilam вҖ” Multiply near base numbers

Steps1) Write deviations 2) Cross-add for left part 3) Multiply deviations for right part
